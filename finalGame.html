The Great Escape

Welcome

You are a typical museum enthusiast, always wanting to learn new things. One day you decide to go an Ancient Artifact Museum which is brand new. You get there only to see that there is hardly anyone at this museum, you brush this observation to the side and begin to explore the many exhibits and rooms they have. All of the sudden the lights go off and all the doors shut, and a mysterious voice comes over the intercom system saying that the museum is currently under quarantine and is being gassed for sanitation purposes. This gas is poisonous and if you inhale to much you will die. It is your job to find your way out the museum before you have inhaled too much of the gas. You can find your way out by finding keys which will unlock doors for you to find your way out.

Do you have what it takes?


How to Play

As you begin your journey to safety in hopes of escaping the library, here are a few tips:
1. Follow the directions carefully.
2. Click on the action you would like to take in the game.
3. You may quit the game at any time by pressing the quit button on the bottom of the page.
